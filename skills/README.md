# The Skills

A library, not a checklist. Each skill is one small, learnable way to handle a recurring part of life, run as a system (a decision you make once) with AI helping only where it earns its place. Steal what fits this season of your life and ignore the rest. Add **one at a time** — see [Start With One Skill](start-with-one.md).

They are grouped by what they *buy you*, not by life category. Pick the payoff you are short on right now.

Every skill page follows the same five parts (what it is, why it's worth it, the one-time setup, where AI earns its place, and how to keep it small). New skills drop in on the same [template](_template.md).

## Closeness — the people you love
The one that compounds the most and gets neglected the most, so it goes first.

| Skill | What it is |
|---|---|
| **[Keep in Touch](keep-in-touch.md)** | Everyone you love on a rhythm; a tool tells you who's overdue. The flagship. |
| Standing dates *(soon)* | One recurring thing on the calendar per close relationship, so there's nothing to initiate. |
| Do a thing together, apart *(soon)* | A shared puzzle or word game you compare notes on. Warmth without small talk. |
| Birthday heads-up *(soon)* | One list with a few days' warning, so you're never scrambling. |
| The forward file *(soon)* | A running note of who loves what, so "saw this, thought of you" writes itself. |
